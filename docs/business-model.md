# Business Model: Community Investigation Services

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-8030`
- ISIC Rev.5: `8030` — investigation activities
- Social impact: due process, fraud deterrence, accountability

## Customer
- law firms and insurers needing case investigation and evidence research
- businesses needing background checks and fraud investigation
- individuals needing licensed investigation services
- programs that cannot accept closed, unauditable case-management
  platforms

## Offer
- investigator licensing and case-authorization scope management
- robotics-assisted surveillance/observation and evidence capture
- case and dispatch records
- evidence and findings reporting
- role-based access and immutable audit ledger

## Revenue
- self-host setup fee
- managed hosting subscription per case/contract
- support retainer with SLA
- surveillance-robot integration and maintenance

## Trust Controls
- a robot/investigator action the governor refuses is never dispatched
- safety-critical actions (surveillance of a private residence, any
  action touching a minor, covert recording) require human sign-off
- an investigator cannot be dispatched outside their verified license
  scope
- evidence reports require source documentation
- sensitive case and personal data stays outside Git
